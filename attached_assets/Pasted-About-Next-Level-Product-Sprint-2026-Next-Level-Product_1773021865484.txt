About Next-Level Product Sprint 2026
Next-Level Product Sprint 2026 is a premier global hackathon engineered for builders, innovators, and visionaries who refuse to stop at the prototype stage. This isn't just a competition it's a proving ground for the next generation of engineers, developers, and product architects who are ready to tackle the problems that define our era.

We live in a world where technology is evolving faster than the systems built to support it. The gap between a clever idea and a production-grade solution has never been more consequential  and that's exactly the gap we're challenging you to close.

Participants will design, develop, and deliver production-ready solutions that address complex, real-world challenges across the most transformative domains in technology  including Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning, cloud computing, scalable data architectures, Web3 & blockchain, cybersecurity, and enterprise-grade application development.

Every submission will be evaluated on technical depth, architectural soundness, innovation, scalability, and real-world deployability. We push teams to go beyond polished demos and build solutions that could genuinely ship, scale, and survive in production environments. With direct mentorship from seasoned industry leaders, access to a global network of exceptional peers, and a platform that rewards bold execution  Next-Level Product Sprint 2026 is where ambition meets impact, and where careers are made.

Whether you're a senior engineer or an ambitious student ready to make your mark on a global stage  this is your moment.

 Tracks
Choose the domain that best aligns with your expertise and vision. Each track has a defined problem space and will be judged by domain experts:

🤖 AI / ML — Build intelligent systems, LLM applications, computer vision pipelines, autonomous agents, or breakthrough ML architectures.

☁️ Cloud Infrastructure — Design scalable, resilient, and cost-efficient cloud-native systems, DevOps pipelines, or edge computing solutions.

📊 Scalable Data Systems — Architect high-throughput data platforms, real-time analytics engines, or next-gen data pipelines.

🔗 Web3 & Blockchain — Create decentralized protocols, smart contract platforms, DeFi applications, or on-chain governance systems.

🔐 Cybersecurity — Develop tools, frameworks, or systems that detect, prevent, or respond to modern security threats at scale.

⚖️ Judging Criteria
Submissions will be evaluated by a panel of industry experts across the following dimensions:

 
Criterion	What We're Looking For
Technical Depth	Sophistication of implementation, use of best practices, and engineering quality
Innovation	Originality of the idea and creativity in the approach
Scalability	Ability to handle real-world load and grow without re-architecture
Real-World Impact	Practical applicability and potential to solve a genuine problem
Deployability	Production-readiness  not just a demo, but something that could actually ship
Presentation	Clarity of documentation, demo quality, and communication of the solution
